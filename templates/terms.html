Terms & Conditions

Please read these before booking. By booking an appointment you agree to the terms below.

1. Booking & appointments

Submitting the booking form is a request for service. We'll reach out to confirm and to go over any details specific to your vehicle if needed. Times are estimates — detailing can run longer or shorter depending on the vehicle's condition.

2. Pricing

Prices shown are starting prices based on the service and vehicle size you select. Heavily soiled vehicles, excessive pet hair, biohazards, severe stains, or other conditions that require extra time or materials may incur an additional charge, which we'll discuss with you before any extra work begins. A travel fee may apply based on how far we travel to reach you.

3. Payment

Payment is collected after the service is complete. We accept cash, card, Venmo, Zelle, Apple Pay, and Cash App.

4. Cancellations & rescheduling

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please give us as much notice as possible. Repeated late cancellations or no-shows may affect future bookings. If we ever have to reschedule on our end (weather, illness, or circumstances beyond our control), we'll work with you to find a new time.

5. Vehicle access & condition

For mobile appointments, please make sure the vehicle is accessible and, where needed, that there's a safe place for us to work along with access to water and power if your service requires it.

6. Personal items

Any personal belongings found in the vehicle are placed in a temporary container during the detail and handed back to you once we're finished.

7. Results & pre-existing conditions

We take great care with every vehicle, but detailing cannot undo pre-existing damage. Scratches, swirl marks, paint defects, stains, oxidation, water spots, odors, and wear that are already present may be improved but cannot always be fully removed. We'll always tell you up front if something is beyond what a given service can correct.

8. Liability

Before we begin, we record a detailed video of the vehicle inside and out, documenting any pre-existing issues. We carry out our work with care using standard, paint-safe products and methods. We are not liable for damage resulting from pre-existing conditions, aftermarket parts, poor prior repairs, fragile or improperly installed components, or mechanical/electrical issues unrelated to our service.

9. Photos & videos

We may take photos and videos of vehicles before, during, and after service, and may use them on our website and social media. License plates can be covered or blurred on request, or you can ask that your vehicle not be posted at all — just let us know.

10. Contact

Questions about these terms? Reach us at contact@luciddetailingva.com or (804) 426-8373.
